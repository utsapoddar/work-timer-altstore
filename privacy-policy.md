# Privacy Policy for Sift

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

Sift ("the app") is a work timer app developed by Utsav Poddar. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Sift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server:

- Session count and streak (used to track progress milestones)
- The date of your last qualifying session (used for streak calculation)
- The file path of a custom ringtone if you choose one

This data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

## Third-Party Services

Sift does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

- **Vibrate** – to vibrate when a phase ends
- **Background processing** – to keep the timer running and sound alarms when the app is in the background

These permissions are used solely to provide the core timer functionality.
