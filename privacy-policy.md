# Privacy Policy for Sift

**Last updated: March 23, 2026**

Sift ("the app") is a work timer app developed by Utsa Poddar. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Sift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Data Storage

All data (timer sessions, settings, sound files) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

Sift does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:
- **Notifications** – to display timer alerts and Live Activities
- **Background processing** – to keep the timer running and sound alarms when the app is in the background

These permissions are used solely to provide the core timer functionality.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
